SARAH LESALE

Floral Designer
Hello, I'm Fleurs Lesale or Sarah ! Do you smell that wonderful smell when you walk into a florist, freshly cut grass or just the intoxicating scent of a rose on your table? These are simple little things, everyday pleasures that we all have in common.

Everything began at the age of 8, amazed by nature, flowers, colors, smells ... I knew ! I wanted to live with flowers all my life and make it my job. To see them gives me an intense happiness and I still have tears in my eyes when I see a mix of flowers on my table or a simple poppy at the edge of the road ...

My dream, I teach it to young people for 11 years now and I am still thirsty to learn every day as my first wonders, then barely 8 years old ... My life is made of flowers, creativity, love and projects, ...

I go to this means of sharing and I try to convey my passion, my job. Making you dream is my first wish, while filling you with my compositions.
